    About Us

    YAGNA DHRUVA 2026 is a flagship techno-cultural fest that brings together innovation, creativity, and competition. The event serves as a platform for students to showcase their technical skills, artistic talents, and collaborative spirit.

    With a diverse range of events, workshops, and competitions, YAGNA DHRUVA aims to inspire learning beyond classrooms while fostering teamwork, leadership, and excellence.

    MrBallen Foundation

    HONORING VICTIMS.
    SUPPORTING FAMILIES.

    The mission of the MrBallen Foundation is to honor victims and support families of heinous crimes through education, training and financial support.
    Real stories have been shared by the MrBallen Foundation to raise awareness and shift public understanding around the impact of violent crime on victims and their families. Training sessions have equipped Partners with expert insights and practical tools to strengthen support for victims and survivors in their care. Has been given by the MrBallen Foundation through Honor Grants, Hope Grants, and Rapid Response Grants to empower partner organizations and deliver support after tragedy.
